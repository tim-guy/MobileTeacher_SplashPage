Our Purpose


Mobile Teacher is the first professional development app built for and by teachers working in developing countries. These educators have desire for professional development, but it may be offered where/when they cannot get to it, provided by external experts whose solutions may not be appropriate for the local context, or not available at all. These educators also have mobile phones, but may lack internet or reliable connectivity. Nevertheless, their phones can be used to democratize their access to professional development, or teacher training, via Mobile Teacher. The digital divide does not have to affect their access to opportunities and those opportunities do not always need to be imposed solutions from external experts.

The app features short video teaching tips of effective classroom practices from teachers working in developing countries. Users can create and submit their own videos for review and posting and/or submit recommended videos by other teachers working in developing countries (see submission guidelines).

The Android app is freely-available on Play and was created following best practices from Google, Android, and UNESCO for designing for the next billion users, or those in developing countries with no, slow, or unreliable connectivity and who may be new internet users. The app uses YouTube Go to accommodate a variety of user connections and respect user budgets for data consumption, allowing videos to be previewed and downloaded before viewing and sharing offline. The app also provides an SMS alternative for those who do not connect to Internet.

Mobile Teacher created a “digital scorecard” based on Google, Android, and UNESCO standards that developers can use when designing for the next billion users (see scorecard).

If you are not a teacher in a developing country, you can still use the app to benefit from the expertise of those who are. The app is also ideal for U.S. Peace Corps Volunteers and English Language Fellows working with host country teachers.

Services

Mobile Teacher is for teachers in developing countries who want good ideas for teaching their students, and want to share their own effective practices. Teachers/users can...

  • Watch videos to see good ideas from other teachers in developing countries
  • Make videos to showcase their own effective practices
  • View and share videos offline without data

    • Contribute

    Requirements

    Your video is…
    • 2 minutes or shorter
    • Shows a teacher who works in a developing country
    • Shows a good idea for teaching
    • Is in clear, simple English or has a transcript file written in English

    Platform

    Mobile Teacher is a free Android app for teachers. You can use it with WiFi or offline.

    Features


    Get good videos about how to teach.

    Requirements


    You need the YouTube Go app to play and download the videos. YouTube Go gives you: Short video preview first. If you like the video, you can watch or download it. Video file size information. Decide if you want to use your data to get the video. Offline sharing. Get more videos from your friends offline.
